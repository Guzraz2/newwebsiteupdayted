Privacy Policy

At Webify Pakistan, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

5. Underage Disclaimer

By using our services, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old, or you have obtained parental consent to use our services. If you are underage, we recommend that you do not submit any personal information without the permission of a legal guardian.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
  • By continuing to use our services after any changes, you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at support@pakwebsolutions.store.
